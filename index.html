SOLEY Suite

Physics-Based PV Yield Engine

From Nanometers to Gigawatts — Outdoors and Indoors

The first photovoltaic software bridging atomic-scale device physics and real-world energy yield. Simulate annual production of theoretical devices (Perovskites, Tandems, OPV) for both outdoor utility-scale and indoor ambient light applications.

The Gap

Device Simulators (SCAPS, etc.) are accurate but too slow for annual yield.
Yield Simulators (PVsyst, etc.) require existing panel datasheets — you cannot simulate what you haven't built.

The SOLEY Bridge

SOLEY uses Bottom-Up Logic: calculates Optical Absorption via TMM and solves Detailed Balance for every timestep.
Result: Change a layer by 10nm and see the impact on Annual Yield (kWh) and LCOE instantly.

Outdoor Simulation

Full utility-scale PV modeling with real meteorological data, tracking systems, and environmental losses.

  • Perez sky model with spectral decomposition
  • 1-axis / 2-axis tracking support
  • Soiling, shading, and aging models
  • LCOE and bankability metrics
Indoor Simulation

World's first physics-based indoor PV yield tool. Realistic room model with view factors and fixture-specific light distribution.

  • View-factor based diffuse light model
  • Anisotropic fixture profiles (LED, fluorescent)
  • Window daylight with spectral transmission
  • Position-dependent irradiance mapping
